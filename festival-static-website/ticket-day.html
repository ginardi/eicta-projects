Regular Day Pass

The Regular Day Pass is perfect if you want to focus on one specific day of Milano Soundwave 2027.

Overview

€79

This pass grants access to the festival site and all stages for a single day (Friday 18, Saturday 19 or Sunday 20 July 2027).

What is included

  • Access to all stages for the selected day
  • Entry to food, drinks and merchandise areas
  • Use of all public facilities (toilets, chill zones, water stations)
  • Entrance valid from 16:00 until 03:00 the next morning

Restrictions

  • Non-transferable ticket: valid for one person only
  • Re-entry not guaranteed after leaving the site
  • Does not include VIP areas or dedicated viewing platforms
