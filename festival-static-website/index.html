Festival Stages

The festival is divided into multiple stages, each with its own capacity, style and location inside Parco Sempione.

Skyline Stage

Max capacity: 15,000

The main stage of Milano Soundwave, located near the central lawn. Designed for headliners, full band performances and large-scale light shows.

Underground Dome

Max capacity: 5,000

A semi-covered dome focused on techno, house and experimental live sets. Late-night visuals and 360° sound system.

City Lights Stage

Max capacity: 4,000

An open stage overlooking the city skyline, dedicated to synth-driven pop and hybrid live / DJ performances.

Garden Stage

Max capacity: 2,500

An intimate area surrounded by trees, ideal for acoustic sets, small bands and sunset sessions.

Practical Information & FAQ

Location

Milano Soundwave 2027 takes place in Parco Sempione, a central park easily reachable by metro (M1 Cairoli or Cadorna), tram and bus. Bike parking areas are available around the park.

Opening Hours

Gates open at 16:00 every day. Last entry at 01:00. Performances follow the official schedule and end around 03:00.

Allowed & Prohibited Items

Small bags, compact cameras and reusable water bottles are allowed. Professional cameras, glass bottles, weapons and fireworks are strictly prohibited.

FAQ

Can I enter with a printed ticket or only digital?

Both are accepted. The QR code on your printed or digital ticket will be scanned at the entrance.

Is there an age limit?

The festival is open to all ages, but guests under 16 must be accompanied by an adult. Alcohol can only be purchased by visitors aged 18 or over.

Do tickets include access to all stages?

Yes, all ticket types include access to every stage. VIP passes simply add dedicated viewing areas and services.
