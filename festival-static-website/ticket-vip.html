VIP Experience

The VIP Experience is designed for the most dedicated fans who want extra comfort, access and services.

Overview

€329

The VIP Experience includes all the benefits of the 3-Day Pass plus access to exclusive areas and priority services inside the festival.

What is included

  • Full 3-day access to all stages
  • VIP viewing platforms at the Skyline Stage
  • Fast-lane entrance at the main gate
  • Access to the VIP lounge with dedicated bar and seating
  • One complimentary welcome drink per day

Restrictions

  • VIP perks are personal and cannot be transferred
  • Dress code smart-casual is recommended for the VIP lounge
  • Misuse of VIP wristband may lead to revocation of VIP benefits
