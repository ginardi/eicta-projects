3-Day Pass

The 3-Day Pass is the best option for enjoying the full Milano Soundwave 2027 experience.

Overview

€199

This pass provides admission to the festival for all three days, from Friday 18 to Sunday 20 July 2027, with a single wristband.

What is included

  • Access to every stage on all three days
  • Re-entry allowed during opening hours
  • Exclusive Milano Soundwave 2027 wristband
  • Access to all food, drinks and merchandise areas

Restrictions

  • Personal wristband must not be removed or damaged
  • Ticket cannot be split across multiple people
  • Does not include VIP platforms or lounges
